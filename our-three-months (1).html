My Love,

Three months. It sounds small when I say it out loud, but it doesn't feel small at all. It feels like a hundred small mornings, a hundred small "good night, love you"s, and somehow it already feels like I can't remember what it was like before you were the first person I wanted to tell things to.

I made you that little board because I wanted you to see, all in one place, how much I've been paying attention. Every photo, every silly note, it's all just me trying to say one big thing in a lot of small ways: I'm really glad it's you.

Here's to the next three months, and the three after that, and however many it takes.

— Yours, always
